About Me

I was born and grew up in the small town of Clarenville, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. I have two brothers: an older brother, Alex, and a younger brother, Tim. We all share a love for science-fiction and fantasy passed down to us by our Mom and Dad. The Lord of the Rings holds a particularly special place in my heart, though I'm also very invested in Dungeons and Dragons. I've always had a love for computers, and even cobbled-together my own through the steady acquisition of new parts over the years, which I installed myself. I've run my computer through its paces, becoming quite the computer-repair-guy (by necessity: I have a bad habit of trying to push it past its limits). More recently, I've finally made the jump from my interests in the hardware side towards the software side, which I plan to make a career in.
